## 💡 Examiner Questions & Answers

**Q: Escrow kya hai?**
A: Third-party jo payment hold karta hai until conditions meet ho jayen.

**Q: Fraud kaise prevent hota hai?**
A: Payment client approval ke baad hi release hoti hai. Manufacturer ko directly nahi milti.

**Q: 5% kyun manufacturer ko pehle?**
A: Incentive hai kaam start karne ka. Initial costs cover hoti hain.

**Q: Agar dispute ho?**
A: Payment history screen mein sab logged hai. Platform mediate kar sakta hai.
